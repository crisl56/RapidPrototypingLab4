# Game Directions #
Starting menu lets you pick a recipe. Only the burger works

after the recpie screen, you can pick your ingredients from a list

then you start preparing your ingredients through a minigame. 
for the lettuce you have to cut it up by clicking the knife repeatedly

then after 25 seconds, you go onto the cooking screen. You try to time the flip of the patty
to the ingame timer. click on the spatula to flip the patty

Then finally the photo screen, you can take a photo of your final product and share it with others
It also saves on your system.
